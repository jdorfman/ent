Security

Being able to secure your website is as important as its design and content. Having a fast website doesn’t mean you should compromise your security. We make sure that your website’s fast, but that it’s also secure using industry-leading encryption standards.

  •   EdgeSecurity

    We generate secure tokens without the wait and deploy them to your users with the same speed with this turnkey solution. Control who sees and who can download your content with digital protection and time sensitive links.

  •   Two Step Authorization

    Protect your users with passwords, as well as a random code that changes every minute that’s tied to their smartphones using Google Authenticator, available for all major smartphone platforms.

  •   EdgeSSL

    Our SSL certificates take seconds to provision and uses our optimized TCP stack, ciphers and hardware — which utilizes Intel Integrated Performance Primitives (IPP), for a 35 percent boost in SSL performance. It is delivered using our network of edge servers. We can also support your existing certificates or have us provision a certificate for you with one click.

  •   Extended API Security

    We go beyond OAuth authentication and offer whitelisting by IP address or CIDR blocks, as well as setting application permissions and limiting access to reporting data, purging privileges and zone management.
